Delete Your Account — Labor Day Pay

App name: Labor Day Pay  ·  Developer: jj  ·  Last updated: April 2026

You can delete your Labor Day Pay account and associated data at any time. There are two ways to do this.

Option 1 — In-app deletion (recommended)

1. Open Labor Day Pay.
2. Go to Settings → Account.
3. Tap Delete account.
4. Confirm. Your account and all cloud-synced data are permanently deleted within 24 hours.

Option 2 — Email request

If you can't access the app, email us and we'll delete your account manually:

📧 rowlow302606@gmail.com

Include the Google account email you used to sign in. We'll confirm deletion within 7 business days.

What gets deleted

  • Your Firebase authentication record (email, user ID)
  • All work day entries, pay records, and settings synced to the cloud
  • Any preferences stored on our servers

What is kept (and for how long)

  • Local device data — if you uninstall the app, any data stored only on your phone is removed with the app. No retention.
  • Anonymized crash logs (if any) — retained by Google Play for up to 90 days for product-improvement purposes, then auto-deleted. Cannot be tied to you after account deletion.
  • Legal records — if you contacted us by email about a dispute, we may retain that correspondence for up to 2 years to comply with applicable law. No app data is included.

After deletion

Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered. You can create a new account at any time by signing in again, but previous data will not be restored.
